Himachal Tour Package

Itinerary

Day 1:- Delhi – Shimla (340 Kms) 7 Hrs.

Upon arrival in Delhi early morning, our representative would meet you and transfer to Shimla. By evening Arrive at Shimla, check in at pre-booked hotel, evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 2:- Shimla (Local Shimla )

This morning after breakfast, we will cover Shimla sightseeing like Kufri, Jakhoo Hills, Voisroi Lodge, Indira Gandhi Tourist Park, Mini Zoo, Chini Bunglow. Evening free to stroll shopping place of Shimla town – The Mall & The Ridge. Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3:-Shimla - Kullu - Manali

This morning after breakfast proceed to Manali. Enroute visit to Hanogi Mata Temple, kullu, Kullu Valley, Sunder Nagar Lake, Shawl Factory, & Pandoh Dam sightseeing. By evening you will reach to Manali, check in at your prebooked Hotel. Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel in Manali.

Day 4:- Manali (Kullu - Manikaran Sightseeing)

Morning after breakfast Proceed to full day Sightseeing of Kullu Valley, Kasol Valley, Rabbit Farm, Shawls factory, and Manikaran Temple Sightseeing. Dinner & Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 5:- Manali - Shimla (250 Kms) .

Morning after breakfast proceeds to Shimla by Deluxe Bus. Arrive Shimla by Evening and check in the hotel. Evening free to visit Mall Road, Ridge, Church, Lakker Bazar. Dinner & Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 6:- Shimla (Shimla, Kufri Sightseeing) .

Morning after breakfast proceed to full day sightseeing of Green Valley, Mini Zoo, Chini Bungalow, Fagu Valley, Kufri, Indira Gandhi tourist park, Fun World and after that catch the Volvo from Shimla to Delhi.
